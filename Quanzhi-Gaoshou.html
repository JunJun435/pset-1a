Info: Ye Xiu is a top-tier pro-player for the game called Glory, an online MMORPG. On the day that Glory launches its 10th server, he was kicked off his team. He decided to keep playing this game by starting all over in the 10th server and build a new team to enter the competitive tournaments. This is a really good chinese anime compared to other ones I've seen and the animations are super clean.
