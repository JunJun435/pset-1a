Isekai Cheat Magician

Genre:

  • Action
  • Adventure
  • Fantasy

Info: This anime all starts out with regular highschool student Taichi Nishimura and Rin Azuma being summoned to another world. The moment they landed in this mysterious world, they were attacked by flesh-eating monsters. Luckily, they were saved by adventurers and taken to a nearby town and to register as an adventurer so that they can find a way to get home. They were tested on their magic capability and shocked everyone with their insane amount of magic. This anime is still on-going at this moment (9/26/2019), this anime is great for you if you enjoy watching an anime with over-powered characters.
