Info:

Ayato Amagiri joins the world's biggest stage for integrated battle intertainment, Rikka academy aka the Asterisk. Ayato plans to find out his sister, Haruka Amagiri's, whereabouts after her disappearance. At the same time, Ayato plans to join the "Star Wars Festival" and decides to partner up Julis Riessfeld. Through countless battles in this tournament, he finds out that 3 seals were placed on his powers by his sister. There are a total of 2 seasons, 12 episodes each, I recommend this Anime to anyone that likes action.
