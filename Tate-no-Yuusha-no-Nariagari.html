Info: 20 years old university student Naofumi Itawanai was summoned to a mysterious world along with 3 other person to be one of the 4 otherworldly heroes. Upon arriving he was framed by the daughter of the kingdoms ruler and was kicked out of the kingdom. Being the shield hero, Naofumi struggles to get stronger and decides to gain companions to fight alongside him. This is one the best anime I've ever seen, seeing Naofumi gets stronger than the 3 other heroes was super hilarious.
